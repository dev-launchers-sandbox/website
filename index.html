Who are we?

We are a group of professional developers and creatives who are passionate about inspiring the next generation of problem solvers, creators, and innovators. With our expertise in the world of technology, we've leveraged the fun of project-based learning to develop effective techniques to teach teens the skills they need to be successful in our increasingly code-centric world, all while doing something they enjoy. We're making video games! At Dev Launchers Labs, we teach our students valuable lessons related to computer science, design, collaboration, problem solving, creative thinking, and most importantly give them the skills and confidence they need to make their dreams a reality.
